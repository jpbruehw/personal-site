All that glitters is gold?

Master's Thesis: Predicting Recessions with Machine Learning

Until recently, logit and probit models have been the gold standard for econometric analysis. However, this is being challenged by more complex and powerful machine learning models. On the other hand, my thesis shows that machine learning models are not better at predicting recessions in a statistically significant way using common econometric metrics, at least for now.
